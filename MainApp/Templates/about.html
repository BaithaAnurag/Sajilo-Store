About Us

Welcome to Sajilo Store — where innovation meets simplicity.

🌟 Our Story

Founded in 2024, we started with a simple idea: to make Hande made Local Product better. What began as a passion project between friends has grown into a small, thriving e-business dedicated to quality, ease, and great customer experiences.

👥 Who We Are

We’re a tight-knit team of creators, thinkers, and doers committed to excellence. We handle everything in-house — from design and customer service to fulfillment and tech — to ensure every experience with us feels personal and professional.

🌍 Our Mission

To build a people-first business that delivers real value, supports ethical practices, and adapts constantly to meet your needs. We believe in transparency, sustainability, and genuine customer relationships.
